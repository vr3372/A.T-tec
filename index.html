MOULDING & MOULD BASICS

by Rajesh Vytla

An injection mould is a die used to mass produce plastic parts. The mould is run in a injection moulding machine to produce parts.
There are different types of moulding based on the type of part required.
A few common types are mentioned below.

TYPES OF MOULDING:

  • Injection moulding
  • Blow moulding
  • Transfer moulding
  • Rotational moulding

Injection moulding:

Injection moulding is a process where molten plastic is injected in to die & is ejected out after cooling.
